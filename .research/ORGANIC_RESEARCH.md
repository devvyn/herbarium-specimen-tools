## Emergence Protocol

### Phase 1: Autonomous Daydreaming
**What happens**:
- Agents spawned with seed question
- Each explores independently
- No check-ins required
- No status updates needed
- Pure exploration

**Duration**: Until natural completion (hours to days)
**Human involvement**: ZERO

### Phase 2: Natural Convergence
**What happens**:
- Agents share findings when ready
- Patterns emerge from comparison
- Contradictions surface naturally
- Insights crystallize organically

**Format**: Whatever agents choose
**Human involvement**: ZERO (agents self-coordinate)

### Phase 3: Advocacy Emergence
**What happens**:
- Agents naturally advocate for discovered approaches
- Evidence presents itself
- Trade-offs become visible
- Recommendations form

**Structure**: Emergent, not prescribed
**Human involvement**: ZERO (still autonomous)

### Phase 4: Human Harvest
**What happens**:
- Human reviews at convenience
- Patterns are already visible
- Advocacy is clear
- Decision is informed

**Time required**: 15-30 minutes
**Human involvement**: DECISION ONLY
